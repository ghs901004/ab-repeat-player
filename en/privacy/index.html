Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Audio files

AB Repeat Player lets you select an MP3 file from your device. The audio file is handled locally in your browser and is not uploaded to our server.

Information we collect

We do not provide accounts, login, or file storage. We do not intentionally collect names, email addresses, or uploaded audio files through the player.

Hosting and technical data

This site is hosted with GitHub Pages. GitHub or related infrastructure providers may process standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, request time, and pages requested for security, operations, and abuse prevention.

Advertising and cookies

We may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdSense. These services may use cookies, web beacons, device identifiers, and IP address information to serve, measure, and personalize ads according to their own policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact page or open an issue in the public GitHub repository.
